Abstract data visualisation through an ecosystem of graphical creatures

Plagnard Hugo/ Simmen Guillaume/ Barbon Federico/ Denic Mersad

Description

We often develop close relationships with data such as cryptocurrency prices, stock market indices or social media metrics. We follow the evolution of our chosen data through the digital ecosystem.

Technology

Data from internet APIs is driving the parameters of the digital environment. With this system, we can represent any kind of data based on quantities.
